I am a Ph.D. Candidate at the [Autonomous Robots Lab](https://www.autonomousrobotslab.com/), [Norwegian University of Science and Technology](https://www.ntnu.edu/). I hold a Bachelor of Science (BSc) degree in Electrical Engineering from the [University of Cyprus](https://www.ucy.ac.cy/?lang=en), Cyprus and a Master of Science (MSc) degree in Automatic Control Systems and Robotics from the [National Technical University of Athens](https://www.ntua.gr/en/), Greece. 

My main area of research is on Autonomous Robots, Unmanned Aerial Vehicles, Multi-Robot Cooperation.
